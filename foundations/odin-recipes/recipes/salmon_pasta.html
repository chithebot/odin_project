Creamy Lemon Salmon Pasta

What Is It?

A luxurious fusion of land and sea, this dish pairs tender, flaky salmon with a velvety cream sauce infused with bright citrus and aromatic garlic

This Italian-inspired favorite is the ultimate comfort food for seafood lovers. Whether you use pan-seared fresh fillets or rich smoked salmon, the star of the dish is the silky, umami-rich emulsion created by combining heavy cream, freshly grated Parmesan, and a splash of starchy pasta water. The addition of lemon zest and juice provides a necessary hit of acidity that "cuts" through the richness of the fish, while fresh herbs like parsley or dill add a garden-fresh finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh fillets of salmon
  • 8 oz penne or fettuccine
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small shallot (diced)
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1 lemon (juiced); lemon zest
  • handful of fresh baby spinach

Steps-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil the Pasta: Cook your pasta in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Sear the Salmon:While the pasta cookes, heat 1 tbsp of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season salmon cubes with salt and pepper and sear until gliden (about 2-3 minutes per side). Remove salmon and set aside.
  3. Build the Base:In the same skillet, melt the butter. Add the shallots and garlic, sauteing until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Create the Cream Sauce:Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until it starts to thicken slightly. Stir in the lemon juice, lemon zest, and Parmesan cheese. If the sauce looks too thick splashin in some of the pasta water.
  5. Toss and Serve:Add the cooked pasta and the salmon back into the skillet. Toss gently to coat. If using spinach, throw it in now; the residual heat will wilt it perfectly.
  6. Garnish:Top with fresh parsley and extra black pepper.
